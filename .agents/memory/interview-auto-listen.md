---
name: Interview Ace auto-listen
description: Interview Ace should auto-start listening after each question and keep the interviewer response conversational.
---

Rule: mock interviews should start listening automatically once the AI question finishes; manual mic taps are only an override, not a requirement.

**Why:** if the candidate has to press the mic for every answer, the experience feels broken and unnatural.

**How to apply:** keep auto-listen active per question, stop it during AI speech/feedback, and let the next prompt re-arm it.

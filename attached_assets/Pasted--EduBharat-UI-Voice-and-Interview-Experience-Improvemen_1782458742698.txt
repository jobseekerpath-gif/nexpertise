## EduBharat UI, Voice and Interview Experience Improvements

### 1. Responsive Layout

1. Redesign every page to fit completely within the browser window without page scrolling on standard desktop screens (1920×1080 and above).

2. Only internal content areas such as chat history or news feeds may scroll. The webpage itself should remain fixed.

3. Automatically adapt all layouts for desktop, laptop, tablet and mobile without breaking the design.

4. Eliminate unnecessary white space and use the available screen width efficiently.

5. Resize cards, sidebars and conversation panels proportionally so the complete working area remains visible.

6. Prevent layout shifting while streaming responses.

---

### 2. Live Interview Experience

1. The AI interviewer must behave like a real HR interviewer instead of a chatbot.

2. The interviewer should:

* Think before asking the next question.
* React naturally to candidate answers.
* Ask intelligent follow-up questions.
* Interrupt politely if the candidate goes completely off-topic.
* Remember previous answers.
* Challenge weak responses.
* Give encouragement naturally.
* Maintain eye contact if avatar is enabled.

3. Every interview should feel different instead of asking questions in a fixed sequence.

4. The interviewer should dynamically change question difficulty based on candidate performance.

5. Detect hesitation, long pauses, filler words and confidence level and adapt the interview accordingly.

6. Remove robotic phrases and repetitive greetings.

7. Add natural fillers like:

* I understand.
* That's interesting.
* Let me ask you something else.
* Could you explain that further?
  only where appropriate.

---

### 3. Continuous Voice Conversation

1. Once the interview starts, keep the microphone active automatically.

2. Never require the candidate to press the microphone button again.

3. Automatically detect when the candidate starts and stops speaking.

4. Begin AI processing immediately after the candidate finishes speaking.

5. Support natural interruptions.

6. If the candidate speaks while AI is talking, pause AI speech, listen to the candidate, and resume naturally.

7. Add configurable silence detection (approximately 1–2 seconds) before processing speech.

8. Provide an always-visible microphone status indicator:

* Listening
* Processing
* Speaking
* Offline

---

### 4. Voice Quality

1. Make both Male and Female voices sound like professional Indian teachers.

2. Use native pronunciation for every supported Indian language.

3. Eliminate robotic pacing.

4. Improve emotion, intonation, pauses and emphasis.

5. Preserve the selected gender throughout the conversation.

6. Switching Male/Female voices should take effect immediately.

### 7. Candidate Experience

Keep the interview screen distraction-free.

Pin the interview controls.

Pin the video/avatar.

Keep only the conversation history scrollable.

Always keep the latest AI question fully visible.

Never scroll the entire webpage automatically.

---

### 8. Performance

Cache reusable data.

Optimize rendering to achieve smooth 60 FPS animations and eliminate UI lag.

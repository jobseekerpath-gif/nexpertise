Live Conversation Voice & UI Improvements

### Voice Quality

1. Make both Male and Female voices sound significantly more natural, expressive, warm, and human-like with realistic breathing, pauses, intonation, and emotion.

2. Replace the current Indian accents with high-quality native Indian voices. Pronunciation should sound like an educated Indian English teacher, not robotic or heavily accented.

3. Improve pronunciation quality for all supported Indian languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese, etc.). The AI must speak each language with a native accent instead of translating English pronunciation.

4. When the selected voice is Male, the AI must always speak using the selected Male voice. When Female is selected, it must immediately switch to the selected Female voice. Do not continue using the previous voice after changing the setting.

5. Voice changes must take effect immediately without requiring a page refresh or restarting the conversation.

6. Keep the same voice throughout the conversation unless the user explicitly changes it.

7. Never mix male and female voices within the same conversation.

8. Support multiple Indian Male and Female voices so users can choose their preferred tutor voice.

9. Improve pronunciation of Indian names, cities, and regional words.

10. Reduce robotic pauses and make conversations sound like a real human tutor.

### Conversation Behaviour

11. If the user switches to another language during conversation, automatically detect it and continue in that language with a native accent.

12. While teaching English, explain difficult words in the student's native language naturally.

13. Maintain the tutor's personality and voice consistency throughout the session.

### UI Improvements

14. The entire Live Conversation page must fit within one browser window on desktop without requiring vertical scrolling at 100% browser zoom (1920×1080 resolution).

15. Keep the layout similar to the current design, but resize panels intelligently so the complete conversation area, controls, and current AI response remain visible.

16. Fix the height of the conversation panel so newly streamed messages remain fully visible without moving the entire page.

17. Only the message area inside the conversation panel may scroll. The overall webpage must remain fixed.

18. Keep the left profile/settings panel fixed while only the conversation panel updates.

19. Remove layout shifts, jumping, and automatic page scrolling while streaming responses.

20. Make the interface fully responsive for desktop, tablet, and mobile while preserving the same overall layout and user experience.

21. On mobile devices, maximize the conversation area while keeping essential controls accessible without excessive scrolling.

22. Ensure the latest AI response is always fully visible inside the conversation panel during streaming.
